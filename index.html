Orphans' Project

Rescue and rehabilitation of orphaned wildlife, so that they can enjoy a life back in the wild when grown.

At the heart of the Sheldrick Wildlife Trust's conservation activities is the
Orphans' Project which has achieved world-wide acclaim through its
hugely successful elephant and rhino rescue and rehabilitation program.
